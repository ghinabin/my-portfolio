About Nabin

I'm a Senior Product Designer with 9+ years of experience crafting user-centered solutions for enterprise healthcare platforms.

Currently at Viveka Health, I architect design systems and lead product design for tools that help employers manage benefits, members find care, and teams coordinate treatment. Before healthcare, I shaped products across aviation, food delivery, and SaaS—learning that great design emerges from deeply understanding both user needs and business constraints.

My approach: start with research, think in systems, design for accessibility, and document everything. I believe the best interfaces feel invisible—they get out of the way and let people accomplish what they came to do.

I hold a Master's in Computer Science from Keele University and certifications in accessible design. When I'm not designing, I contribute subsidized design services to student publications—passionate about making quality design accessible to those just starting out.

What I Bring

Strategic Thinking

I connect user needs to business outcomes, ensuring design decisions drive measurable impact.

Systems Approach

I build scalable foundations—design systems, component libraries, documentation—that multiply team effectiveness.

Research Rigor

I ground decisions in evidence: user interviews, usability testing, data analysis.

Accessibility Focus

I design for everyone, baking WCAG compliance into the process rather than treating it as an afterthought.

Prototyping & Execution

Expert in Figma, Adobe XD, and modern design tools. I create high-fidelity prototypes that communicate intent clearly.

Collaborative Leadership

I mentor junior designers, facilitate cross-functional alignment, and translate between design and engineering.
